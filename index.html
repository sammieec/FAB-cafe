Welcome to Fancy A Brew Coffee Shop and Café







Our homely coffee shop and café is located in the heart of Manchester where we have been serving quality products and excellent customer service for over 5 years. We pride ourselves on our welcoming atmosphere where anyone can relax and really enjoy a break from the hustle and bustle of life.

We offer a wide range of hot and cold drinks all made by our trained barista’s who can help you choose the perfect drink, whatever you’re in the mood for. Our menu offers a range of cakes, desserts and pastries that all homemade by our very talented bakers as well as options for a balanced breakfast or a light lunch.

In a rush? Not to worry…we’ve got a dedicated checkout for takeaways so you can still get your favourite drinks and food when you’re in a rush. Why not preorder through the website for an even quicker experience.
